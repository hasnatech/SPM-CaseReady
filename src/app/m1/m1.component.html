Customer Facts
  • Green Grocers is located in an area that makes hiring challenging & they struggle with turnover in the meat department. Meat cutting is done 5 times weekly and takes about 6 hours each time. It also has to be performed by an employee that requires special training
  • The butcher program takes about 10 hours each week for an assistant manager to track inventory and determine what meat needs to be ordered, cut, etc
  • Green Grocers estimates that they could increase sales by .02% if they could predict when products would run out and avoid items running out of stock. A major concern of managers is that they fear they lose sales due to long wait times
  • Shrinkage (theft/spoilage) costs the client about 6% of sales
  • Their current butcher program requires a significant amount of space in the backroom and cooling storage. If they utilized case ready, they could reduce their current packaging budget of $.65/lb
  • While not every cut is being used by Green Grocers, they do make some profits from their trimmings, which comprise about 3.3% of volume purchased & are sold at $.87/lb, on average
